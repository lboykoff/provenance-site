01 The standard What every
Provenance product
is built on

A claim is not evidence. We build hiring around the difference.

Résumés, cover letters, portfolios and interview answers are all claims. They always were. What changed is that producing a convincing claim now costs nothing, so the signal that used to separate candidates has mostly stopped working.

We do not try to detect AI. Detection guesses, and a wrong guess rejects a real person for writing well. That is a worse failure than the one it prevents.

Instead every system we build moves the decision onto something you can look at directly: an artifact, a short sample of the actual work, questions only someone who did the thing can answer. That principle is the through-line in everything we make, whatever the role or the format.
